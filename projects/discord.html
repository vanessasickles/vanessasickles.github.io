Discord, in my book, is an awesome company that has made my online communication with others seamless. They're witty and in-touch with their userbase, and provide awesome text, voice, and video chat service–for free. However, you can choose to support them by purchasing Discord Nitro, which gives you some neat perks.

Experimenting with animations that enhance the experience of a user interface, I aimed to transform the still page that advertised Nitro into something with a little more spice. I wanted to give the user a pleasantly-surprising experience that would energize them to buy, so I turned to animating their pre-existing imagery to do so!
